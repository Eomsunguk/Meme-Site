ABOUT MEME PULSE

What this site does

Meme Pulse is a structured archive of public meme posts. The site curates a compact weekly snapshot by category, adds short summaries and tags, and preserves older snapshots for comparison.

Why it exists

The goal is to make meme trends easier to browse than a raw stream. Visitors can scan categories, search tags, and jump into archived weeks without repeating the same search on multiple platforms.

How items are presented

Each card includes the meme title, a short summary, category context, and a link to the original source. The archive is intended for discovery and reference, not for replacing the source platform.

Editorial focus

The site prioritizes clarity over volume. It avoids creating many thin destination pages with near-duplicate layouts and instead keeps the archive consolidated into useful browsing sections.

Review mindset

Meme Pulse is designed to behave like a browsable reference layer. Pages should explain category purpose, show source context, and offer enough original guidance to stand without relying on ads.
