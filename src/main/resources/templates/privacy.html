PRIVACY SUMMARY

Privacy and external links

Meme Pulse displays categorized archive data and links visitors to external source platforms. This page explains that relationship in plain language.

Site data

The site stores archive metadata needed to render categories, weekly snapshots, and source references. It is not positioned as a user account system or a community platform.

External destinations

When you click a source link, you leave this site and move to a third-party platform. Those platforms apply their own policies, cookies, and account rules.

Advertising note

If advertising is added later, this page should be expanded with the exact ad technology, cookie disclosures, and user-choice language used on the live production domain.

Review expectation

Privacy information is part of site trust, but it does not replace content quality. The core archive pages still need to remain useful even when a visitor never interacts with ads.
