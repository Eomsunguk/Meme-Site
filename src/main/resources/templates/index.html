SITE STANDARDS

Quality signals this site now makes explicit

Distinct content purpose

Meme Pulse does not create multiple thin pages around the same topic. The homepage combines search, category browsing, and archives in one clear destination.

Transparent source handling

Items include summaries and source attribution so users understand what they are clicking before leaving the site.

Visitor-first navigation

Important sections are reachable from the top navigation and footer, and informational pages explain the site, privacy notes, and contact path.

Ads follow content, not the reverse

Advertising is reserved for pages that already deliver clear informational value. Utility pages and low-context destinations should stay secondary.

FAQ

Common visitor questions

Are these real-time meme rankings?

No. This site publishes scheduled weekly snapshots. The update date is shown at the top of the page.

Why link to original sources?

The archive is meant to help discovery and comparison. Original links let visitors verify context and continue browsing from the source platform.

What makes this different from a raw feed?

Items are grouped, tagged, summarized, and preserved over time so trends are easier to revisit without searching across multiple communities manually.
